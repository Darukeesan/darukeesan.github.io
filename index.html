About Me...

I am currently a Lecturer (Probationary) in the Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering at the Faculty of Engineering, University of Jaffna, Sri Lanka. I completed my undergraduate study at University of Moratuwa, with a major in Electronic and Telecommunication Engineering. I worked as an Academic Instructor and later as an Assistant Lecturer in the Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering.
